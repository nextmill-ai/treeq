## Section 4 — PHCrx-only: Skip for TreeQ
These PHCrx species are irrelevant to Rochester / upstate NY removal work:
- **All palms** (17 species) — Sabal, Phoenix, Washingtonia, Syagrus, Cocos, Butia, etc.
- **Tropical fruit trees** — Mango, Avocado, Citrus, Pomegranate, Olive
- **Southwest desert trees** — Desert Willow, Blue Palo Verde, Arizona Cypress
- **Pacific coast oaks** — Coast Live Oak, Live Oak
- **Giant Sequoia, Dawn Redwood** — ornamental only at this latitude
- **Longleaf Pine** — southeastern US only
- **Crape Myrtle** — Zone 7+, marginal at best in ROC
